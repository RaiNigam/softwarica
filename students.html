Student Visa Information

The Department of Immigration issues student visas to foreign students pursuing academic /non academic degree and research in Nepal, upon the recommendation of line agency (Ministry of Education). Visas are also issued for teachers/professors who are involved as an academician (teaching) in Nepal, upon the recommendations from the concerned agencies of Nepal government.

The necessary documents to apply for a visa are as follows:

  • Recommendation letter from Ministry of Education
  • University /College Admission Receipt (original)
  • Bank Statement (latest one week) with sufficient funds.
  • Online Application form ( Link to Online Study Visa Application)
  • Copies of passport and latest Nepali Visa
  • Marriage Certificate and Birth Certificate ( for those applying their spouse and children as dependents)
  • Progress report Card (for renewal of Student Visa)
